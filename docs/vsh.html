Definitions & Formulas

Orthonormal basis functions on S², essential for solving the Laplace equation and decomposing angular fields.

Spherical Harmonics

Yℓm(θ,φ) form an orthonormal basis on S². Here Pℓ|m| are the associated Legendre polynomials.

Orthonormality

Integration over the unit sphere yields a Kronecker delta — the functions are mutually orthogonal and unit-normalised.

Vector Spherical Harmonics

Vector-valued generalisations constructed from scalar SH and gradient operators — the basis for electromagnetic and fluid applications.

Eigenvalue Equation

Spherical harmonics are eigenfunctions of the angular Laplacian (Laplace–Beltrami operator) with eigenvalue −ℓ(ℓ+1).

Addition Theorem

Connects SH of two directions to the Legendre polynomial of their relative angle — key for convolution on the sphere.

Hydrogen Wavefunction

The angular part of the hydrogen electron wavefunction is exactly a spherical harmonic — the visualizer shows this directly.
